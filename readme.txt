Allows Netbookings businesses to easily make Wordpress posts that contain showcases of their services or packages.

== Description ==

Collection of shortcodes to present your Netbookings offerings.

Simply and easily create posts that show off your best Netbookings services and packages. The showcase will include an image, description and 'Book' or 'Gift' buttons as appropriate.
You may choose from a variety of styling options to make the showcase match your website.

You can also show your bathing or entry availability in an easy to use chart, presenting times available for guests to book.

Note: 
This plugin will provide an external link to your Netbookings business via your business ID. 
For more information on the Netbookings privacy policy see: http://netbookings.com.au/home/privacy-policy/

== Installation ==

1. Contact us informing us that you would like to use this plugin. We will transfer your packages and services data and guide you through the installation process.

== Screenshots ==

1. Easily present your package or service when creating a post.

2. Conveniently view when your packages and and services were last updated from your Wordpress backend. 

3. Present your bathing availability on your website.
